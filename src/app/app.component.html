this app demonstrates using the turf.js difference method to figure out the percentage of coverage.

the tests starts with a 100 by 100 meter square located at LatLon coordinates 0,0. This means that the total area is about 10,000 meter squared plus a few for curvature given we get these from LatLonSphericals

below you can define the parameters for a new rectangle that will represent area covered,(currently centered at 0,0). Once defined, you can click calc to find the difference left as well as the ratio currently covered

You can define multiple shapes in sequence to see how much is covered when they are combined. Hit reset shape to remove all coverage and restore the original area
